Alden Library artists' books collection metadata application profile

Introduction

This Metadata Application Profile (MAP) is intended to guide cataloging of the artists' books collection at Ohio University Libraries' Alden Library. The artists' books collection is a collaboration between Ohio University' Fine Arts Library and the Mahn Center for Archives and Special Collections to build a collection of artists' books. Artists' books are works of art that use the form of the book, and often have material, technical, and other attributes that are of interest to researchers but are not accounted for in a standard MARC/RDA or AACR2 bibliographic record. This MAP is intended to provide a consistent way to provide access to these attributes.

Instructions

This MAP takes the form of a checklist with examples.

  1. Catalog the artists' book according to standard bibliographic practice.
  2. Enhance access to the record by ensuring that the MARC fields listed below are filled per their instructions.

Glossary

  • AAT: The Getty Research Institute's Art & Architecture Thesaurus, code aat.
  • LCGFT: the Library of Congress Genre/Form Terms, code lcgft.
  • RBMS Controlled Vocabularies: The ALA's Rare Book and Manuscripts Section's set of six controlled vocabularies:
    • Binding Terms (code rbbin)
    • Genre Terms (code rbgenr)
    • Paper Terms (code rbpap)
    • Printing & Publishing Evidence (code rbpri)
    • Provenance Evidence (code rbprov)
    • Type Evidence (code rbtyp)

Fields

100 Main Entry--Personal Name

  • Record RDA creator role in subfield ǂe, frequently book artist.

100 1_ Barton, Carol June, ǂd 1954- ǂe book artist.

300 Physical Description

  • Follow RDA instruction 3.4.1.3 Recording Extent
  • Record the dimensions of the artists' book in subfield ǂc in the from of height by width by depth in centimeters separated by x as if it were a box.

300 __ 12 unnumbered pages : ǂb illustrations ; ǂc 23 x 13 cm + ǂe [6] illustrated cards

340 Physical Medium

500 General Note

520 Summary, Etc

562 Copy and Version Identification Note

563 Binding Information

590 Local Note

  • Record edition numbers and author's signatures in this field using the formula Alden Special Collections copy is number X of Y [and is signed by author].

590 __ Alden Special Collections copy is number 9 of 500 and is signed by the author.

590 __ Alden Special Collections copy is number 6 of 25.

590 __ Alden Special Collections copy is signed by the author.

655 Index Term--Genre/Form

  1. Record all aspects of the artists' book covered by LCGFT using LCGFT terms. Use 2nd indicator 7 and subfield ǂ2 lcgft.
  2. Record all aspects of the artists' book not covered by LCGFT using AAT terms. Use 2nd indicator 7 and subfield ǂ2 aat.
  3. Record all aspects of the artists' book not covered by LCGFT and AAT using the RBMS Controlled Vocabularies terms. Use 2nd indicator 7 and the appropriate subfield ǂ2 code.

710 Added Entry--Corporate Name

  • Record the publisher of the artists' book.

Deprecated Fields

654 Subject Added Entry--Faceted Topical Terms

This field seems to have been devised for AAT faceting, but does not seem to be well documented, used or supported by most OPACs.

653 Index Term--Uncontrolled

655 _4 Index Term--Genre/Form

The 655 field with 2nd indicator 4 is not loaded or indexed by ALICE.
